How to get there?

By Bus

You can take a bus from Manila to Bolinao. Several bus companies operate routes to Bolinao from different terminals in Manila, such as Victory Liner, Five Star Bus, and Dagupan Bus. The bus ride usually takes around 5 to 6 hours, depending on traffic conditions.

By Car

By Private Vehicle: If you're driving from Manila, take the North Luzon Expressway (NLEX) and continue onto the Subic-Clark-Tarlac Expressway (SCTEX). From SCTEX, take the Tarlac-Pangasinan-La Union Expressway (TPLEX) and exit at Pozorrubio. Follow the signs leading to Alaminos City, then proceed to Bolinao.


Travel tips


  • Before your trip, check the weather forecast for Bolinao to ensure you're prepared for any weather conditions. While the cave itself provides shelter, you'll want to be ready for any outdoor activities or transportation.
  • he terrain around the Enchanted Cave may be rocky or uneven, so wear comfortable and sturdy footwear that's suitable for walking or hiking.
  • While there may be amenities available near the cave entrance, it's always a good idea to bring your water and snacks, especially if you plan to spend a lot of time exploring or waiting for your turn.
  • Bolinao can be hot and sunny, especially during the midday hours. Bring sunscreen, a hat, sunglasses, and lightweight clothing to protect yourself from the sun's rays.
  • Drink plenty of water, especially if you'll be spending time outdoors or engaging in physical activities. Dehydration can sneak up on you, so keep a water bottle handy and take regular breaks to hydrate.
  • The Enchanted Cave is a natural wonder, so be sure to respect the environment and follow any rules or guidelines provided by the staff. Avoid littering, stay on designated paths, and refrain from touching or damaging any rock formations.
  • While some establishments near the cave entrance may accept credit or debit cards, it's always a good idea to bring some cash for entrance fees, snacks, souvenirs, or other expenses.
  • If you opt for a guided tour of the cave, listen to your guide's instructions and follow any safety precautions they provide. They're knowledgeable about the cave's features and can enhance your experience with interesting insights and stories.

About Enchanted Cave

History of Enchanted Cave

The Enchanted Cave, like many limestone caves, formed over thousands of years through geological processes such as erosion and dissolution. The dissolution of limestone by acidic water gradually carved out the cave's chambers, passages, and unique rock formations, including stalactites and stalagmites.

The cave's name, "Enchanted," evokes a sense of mystery and wonder. According to local folklore, the cave was once believed to be inhabited by mystical beings or enchanted creatures, lending it an otherworldly charm. Stories of mythical creatures and supernatural occurrences add to the cave's mystique and have been passed down through generations.
