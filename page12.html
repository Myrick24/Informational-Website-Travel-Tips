Our Farm Republic

Our Farm Republic is an integrated organic farm located in Mangatarem, Pangasinan, Philippines. It covers 5.6 hectares and is owned by the Santiago family. The farm is dedicated to promoting agri-tourism and organic agriculture.

About Our Farm Republic

History of Our Farm Republic

Our Farm Republic was founded by the Santiago family with a vision to promote organic farming, agri-tourism, and sustainable agriculture. The farm was established as a model for environmentally conscious farming practices.

From its inception, Our Farm Republic has been dedicated to cultivating organic produce using natural and sustainable methods. The farm emphasizes the importance of soil health, biodiversity, and chemical-free farming techniques.
