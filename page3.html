How to get there?

By Bus

If you want to commute from Manila to Bolo Beach, you can ride a bus that goes to Alaminos City, Pangasinan. You may proceed to the following bus terminals and ride any of these buses:

  • You can take a bus from Manila to Alaminos City, Pangasinan. Several bus companies operate routes to Alaminos City from different terminals in Manila, such as Victory Liner, Five Star Bus, and Dagupan Bus.

    • Regular or non-air-conditioned buses: Approximately PHP 350 to PHP 500 per person. Air-conditioned buses: Approximately PHP 400 to PHP 700 per person.


    By Car

    If you have access to a private vehicle or plan to rent one, you can drive from Manila to Alaminos City. The journey usually takes around 4 to 6 hours, depending on traffic and road conditions. From Alaminos City, you can follow signs or use GPS to navigate to Barangay Bolo and then to Bolo Beach.


    Once you arrive in Alaminos City, you can take a tricycle or jeepney to Barangay Bolo, where Bolo Beach is located.


    Travel tips


    • Research the route from your starting point to Bolo Beach, including transportation options, accommodations, and activities. Make reservations for accommodations if needed, especially during peak seasons.
    • Bring essentials such as sunscreen, hats, sunglasses, insect repellent, swimwear, towels, and comfortable clothing. Don't forget any medications or personal items you may need.
    • Bring plenty of water to stay hydrated, especially if you're spending time under the sun. It's also a good idea to bring snacks or light refreshments.
    • You may bring extra clothes if you want to swim or do water activities.
    • If you're traveling by public transportation, pack light to make your journey more comfortable. Consider using a backpack or a lightweight suitcase for easy mobility.
    • Follow the protocols set by the tourism office.
    • Respect the natural surroundings of Bolo Beach by disposing of trash properly and avoiding activities that may harm the environment. Leave only footprints and take only memories.
    • Do not leave any trash and keep the environment clean at all times.

    About Bolo Beach

    History of Bolo Beach

    Bolo Beach is situated along the coastline of Pangasinan, a province with a rich history dating back to pre-colonial times. The province is known for its numerous beaches, islands, and coastal attractions, which have attracted visitors for centuries. In recent decades, Pangasinan has seen significant growth in tourism, with Bolo Beach becoming one of its popular destinations. Local government and private sector initiatives have contributed to the development of tourism infrastructure, accommodations, and amenities to cater to visitors.
